# Displacement

The change in an object's [[Position]]
$$
\Delta \vec{d} = \vec{d}_{final} - \vec{d}_{initial}
$$
$$
\Delta \vec{d}_{total} = \vec{d}_{1} + \vec{d}_{2} + \dots
$$

If the direction is the same, then add, otherwise subtract
