# Aquinas and the Highest Good + Desire for Good
St. Thomas Aquinas insisted that ethics comes from the end inscribed in the nature of all creatures. A person’s core is the desire for the good, and God is considered the highest good. People live a good life by using their intelligence and capabilities, following natural law.

# 4 Gospel Writers and Who They Wrote For
- **Matthew**: Wrote for the Jewish People. Portrayed Jesus as a teacher, the new Moses.  
- **Mark**: Wrote for the Romans and Gentiles. Portrayed Jesus as a man in a hurry, earthly and easy to relate to.  
- **Luke**: Wrote for the Gentiles. Portrayed Jesus as compassionate and forgiving.  
- **John**: Wrote for everyone. Portrayed Jesus as noble, majestic, and divine.
