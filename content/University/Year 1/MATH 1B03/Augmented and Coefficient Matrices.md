The linear system
$$
\begin{equation}
\begin{cases}
2y = -4 \\
2x + 6y + 2z = 4 \\
3x + z = 7
\end{cases}
\end{equation}
$$
becomes the augmented matrix
$$
\begin{bmatrix}
0 & 1 & 0 & -4 \\
2 & 6 & 2 & 4 \\
3 & 0 & 1 & 7
\end{bmatrix}
$$ with $m*n = 3 * 4$ (3 rows and 4 columns)
